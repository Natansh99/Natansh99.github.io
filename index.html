About

Hear hear, Game Of Thrones’ fans! , because we have some great news for you. With the final season around the corner, we feel that there is an ardent need for a strong Game of Thrones community to bid farewell to the world's best TV series ever, one last time.
So, clear your calendars for April 15th. As for the first time in Indore Cafe Westeros, a GoT themed cafe in Indore's Mahalaxmi Nagar is organizing screening of the latest episode of this fantasy show which is based on the books by George R.R. Martin. (In nominal rate).
So come and lets discuss theories, predictions and strategies and of course root for your favorite GOT characters!
Apart from the screening, this cafe is offering amazing deals on food and drinks, and guess what - they have a special GoT themed menu as well. How cool is that?
So if you think you are one of the chosen few, please fill up the form to become the club member as we are planning to do live screenings of the GoT episodes, quizzes and much more. Also stand a chance to win cash prize.
Valar Morghulis !!
